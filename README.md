# Superstore Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Superstore sales data to evaluate revenue, profitability, customer segments, and regional performance. The goal is to identify business insights that support data-driven decision-making and profit optimization.

## Key Business Questions
- Which regions and categories generate the highest profit?
- How do discounts impact revenue and margins?
- Which products drive losses?
- What customer segments contribute most to sales?

## Key Insights
- Identified high-revenue and low-profit product categories
- Analyzed regional sales trends and profit distribution
- Evaluated discount impact on profitability
- Highlighted opportunities for pricing and inventory optimization

## Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Jupyter Notebook
- Excel

## Outcome
Delivered actionable business insights to improve pricing strategy, sales planning, and profitability.
